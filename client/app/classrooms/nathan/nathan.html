Bio

Nathan Murray is a graduate of U.C.S.B. and S.S.U. He currently holds three certifications in education: Multiple Subject Credential, Physical Education Supplemental Authorization and a Montessori Degree from N.A.M.C. He approaches learning in a positive, organic and creative way. Nathan wants each student to gain some "grit" in their pursuit of knowledge. "I believe when a student is steadily challenged they become more engaged in their own learning process. Also, I want them to know the HOW and WHY of learning and not just the work itself." He practices the Montessori philosophy by acting as a guide and by looking at each learner as an individual with very diverse needs and motivations.
