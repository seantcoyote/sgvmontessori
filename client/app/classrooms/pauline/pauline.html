Bio

Pauline Hope lives in Inverness with her husband George and their cat Batmilk. She discovered her passion to teach while serving as a school garden instructor. Pauline has a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Sonoma State University, a Multiple Subjects Credential, and an Art Credential. Pauline enjoys applying her Montessori training and watching students thrive in the learning environment. In her spare time, she loves to ride mountain bikes and make art.
